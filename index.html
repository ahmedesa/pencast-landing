FAQ

Common questions

Does PenCast work with Photoshop and Clip Studio?

Yes. PenCast injects stylus events directly into macOS and Windows so Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Figma, and any drawing app see the S Pen as a real Wacom tablet with full pressure and tilt.

Which Samsung devices work?

Any Samsung Galaxy with a built-in S Pen — Galaxy S Ultra series, Galaxy Note series, Galaxy Z Fold series, and Galaxy Tab S series.

What is the latency?

USB: ~5ms. WiFi on a good local network: under 20ms — comparable to professional wired drawing tablets.

Can teachers use PenCast for presentations?

Yes. The transparent overlay lets you draw on top of any open window. Laser pointer and highlighter are free — no Pro needed.

Is PenCast better than a Wacom?

PenCast turns a device you already own into a drawing tablet — saving you $80–$200. Full pressure, tilt, hover, and palm rejection at a fraction of the cost.

How does the connection work?

Three modes: WiFi with auto-discovery via QR code, USB via ADB for ultra-low latency, and Bluetooth BLE (Pro) for wireless use without WiFi.
